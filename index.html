About JK Stays

Your Private Escape in the Western Ghats

Set amidst a lush areca nut and coffee plantation, JK Stays offers an authentic homestay experience with traditional Coorg hospitality. Whether you're here for a quiet weekend or a group celebration, the forest is your backyard.
